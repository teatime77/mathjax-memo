The Matrix Cookbook

1 Basics

$$ \newcommand{\det}{\mathrm{det}} \\ \newcommand{\tri}{\mathrm{tri}} \\ \newcommand{\A}{\m{A}} \\ \newcommand{\B}{\m{B}} \\ \newcommand{\X}{\m{X}} \\ \newcommand{\Y}{\m{Y}} $$ $$ (\bm{A B})^{-1} = \bm{B}^{-1} \bm{A}^{-1} \\ (\m{A B})^{-1} = \m{B}^{-1} \m{A}^{-1} \\ (\mathrm{A B})^{-1} = \mathrm{B}^{-1} \mathrm{A}^{-1} \\ (\mathbf{A B})^{-1} = \mathbf{B}^{-1} \mathbf{A}^{-1} \\ (A B)^{-1} = B^{-1} A^{-1} \\ \tag{1} $$ $$ (\m{A}\T)^{-1} = (\m{A}^{-1})\T \\ \tag{3} $$ $$ (\m{A} + \m{B})\T = \m{A}\T + \m{B}\T \\ \tag{4} $$ $$ (\m{A B})\T = \m{B}\T \m{A}\T \\ \tag{5} $$

1.1 Trace

$$ \Tr(\m{A}) = \Tr(\m{A}\T) \tag{13} $$ $$ \Tr(\A \B) = \Tr(\B \A) \tag{14} $$ $$ \Tr(\A + \B) = \Tr(\A) + \Tr(\B) \tag{15} $$

1.2 Determinant

$$ \det(\A\T) = \det(\A) \tag{20} $$ $$ \det(\A \B) = \det(\A) \det(\B) \tag{21} $$ $$ \det(\A^{-1}) = 1 / \det(\A) \tag{22} $$

2 Derivatives

$$ \partial(\alpha \X) = \alpha \partial \X \tag{34} $$ $$ \partial(\X + \Y) = \partial \X + \partial \Y \tag{35} $$ $$ \partial(\Tr(\X)) = \Tr(\partial \X) \tag{36} $$ $$ \partial(\X \Y) = \partial(\X) \Y + X \partial(\Y) \tag{37} $$ $$ \partial(\X\T) = (\partial \X)\T \tag{44} $$
