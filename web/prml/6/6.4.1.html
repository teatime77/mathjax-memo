6.4.1 線形回帰再訪

$\x$を入力ベクトル、$\w$を$M$次元の重みベクトルとして $$ y(\x) = \w\T \v{\phi}(\x) \tag{6.49} $$ $\w$の事前分布は平均が0の等方的なガウス分布とする。 $$ p(\w) = /N(\w | \v{0}, \alpha^{-1}\m{I}) $$ $$ \vv{y} = \m{\Phi}\w \tag{6.51} $$ $\m{\Phi}$は計画行列。 $$ \Phi_{nk} = \phi_k(\x_n) $$ $$ \E[\vv{y}] = \E[\m{\Phi}\w] = \m{\Phi} \E[\w] = \v{0} \tag{6.52} $$ $$ \mathrm{\vv{y}} = \E[\vv{y} \vv{y}\T] = \E[\m{\Phi}\w (\m{\Phi}\w)\T] = \m{\Phi} \E[\w \w\T ] \m{\Phi}\T = \frac{1}{\alpha} \m{\Phi} \m{\Phi}\T = \m{K} \tag{6.53} $$ ここで$\m{K}$はグラム行列で $$ K_{nm} = k(\x_n, \x_m) = \frac{1}{\alpha} \phi(\x_n)\T \phi(\x_m) \tag{6.54} $$ $$ \E[y(\x_n)y(\x_m)] = k(\x_n, \x_m) \tag{6.55} $$ $$ k(x, x') = \exp(- \theta |x - x'|) \tag{6.56} $$
