2.3

$$ \binom{N}{n} + \binom{N}{n-1} = \frac{N!}{n!(N-n)!} + \frac{N!}{(n-1)!(N+1-n)!} \\ = \frac{(N+1-n)N!}{n!(N+1-n)!} + \frac{nN!}{n!(N+1-n)!} \\ = \frac{(N+1)!}{n!(N+1-n)!} = \binom{N+1}{n} \tag{78} $$
