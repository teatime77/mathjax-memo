(2.213)の証明

(2.212)から $$ \exp(\eta_k) = \frac{\mu_k}{1 - \sum_j \mu_j} $$ $$ \mu_k = \exp(\eta_k) (1 - \sum_j \mu_j) \tag{1} $$ $k$について足し合わせて $$ \sum_k \mu_k = \sum_k \exp(\eta_k) (1 - \sum_j \mu_j) $$ $$ (1 + \sum_k \exp(\eta_k)) \sum_j \mu_j = \sum_k \exp(\eta_k) $$ $$ \sum_j \mu_j = \frac{\sum_j \exp(\eta_j)}{(1 + \sum_j \exp(\eta_j))} $$ これを(1)式に代入して $$ \mu_k = \exp(\eta_k) (1 - \frac{\sum_j \exp(\eta_j)}{(1 + \sum_j \exp(\eta_j))}) \\ = \frac{\exp(\eta_k)}{1 + \sum_j \exp(\eta_j)} \tag{2.213} $$
